Oh, hello there! I'm Lynxmic, based in Europe. In addition to video creation and other interests (i.e. tech), I've also been maintaining my own personal blogs, be it on Blogger or WordPress, or even Medium. Since 2021, I've been behind this current blog, which was made possible with the help of Jekyll, and hosted through GitHub Pages, independent from any of the most popular blogging platforms.


Anyways, this is the place where I occasionally write about stuff in long form, from topics in the area of my interests, to updates 'n stuff.


With a decade of experience in making YT videos as a hobby, I've been - since 2016 - putting time and effort into the Lynxmic project, learning a lot in the process, and currently, the main channel sits at 200+ subscribers (as of May 2023).


I'm not really the best at coming up with an "introduction" for an about page on a blog, so what I can say is just have fun browsing around.
