Notifications

Ways to get notified when there's a new blog post.


The Lynxmic Blog embraces RSS! The blog has an official feed located at {{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/{{ site.rss }} to which you can subscribe to with your preferred RSS reader.

Provided you're in the Lynxmic Discord Server, you can choose to have new blog posts delivered to you through Discord pings/mentions as well. Just navigate to the role-assign channel and select New Blog Posts.
